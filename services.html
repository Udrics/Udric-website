What We Do

We specialise in design
that gives presence.

Our Offering

Comprehensive interior
design & consultancy.

Udric is a full-service interior design studio passionate about giving every space a presence. We specialise in comprehensive interior design, lighting consultancy, and turnkey installations — always rooted in the belief that great design is felt, not just seen.

Whether fitting out a commercial office, designing a luxury home, or crafting the ambience of a hospitality venue — we bring the same intentional, minimalist touch to every project.

Our Expertise

What we're known for.

01

Residential Design

Transforming homes into deeply personal spaces. From concept to completion, we design interiors that reflect who you are — with an aesthetic that whispers rather than shouts.

02

Commercial Design

Offices, studios, and retail spaces that project your brand with confidence. We design workspaces that energize teams and leave lasting impressions on every visitor.

03

Lighting Design

Minimalist light fixtures, ambient systems, and statement chandeliers that sculpt a room's mood. Light is our most powerful tool — and we use it with precision.

04

Hospitality Design

Hotels, restaurants, lounges, and spas that guests never want to leave. We design hospitality spaces around the art of feeling — because experience is everything.

05

Space Consultancy

Not every project needs a full redesign. Sometimes it needs a trained eye and an honest conversation. Expert consultancy for spaces at any stage.

06

Turnkey Installation

From sourcing to final nail — we manage the entire project end to end. Full accountability, obsessive quality control, and delivery that always exceeds the brief.

How We Work

Our process.

01

Discover

We listen first. We learn about you, your space, your goals, and what presence means to you — before we sketch a single line.

02

Design

Our team creates concepts that are both beautiful and deeply intentional. Every element earns its place. Nothing placed without purpose.

03

Refine

We work with you through every iteration until the design feels exactly right — nothing more, nothing less than what the space needs.

04

Deliver

Execution is where we prove ourselves. We oversee every detail from procurement to final installation with complete accountability.

Our Origin

The story behind
the name Udric.

Udric was created from two names — Udeh and Patric. But its meaning runs far deeper than the people it came from.

In Igbo, Udeh means presence. Some call it "cream." That word — cream — unlocks everything we stand for. Cream is small, almost invisible in the hand, yet when applied to the skin, it transforms. It softens. It reveals beauty. It gives life.

That is precisely what Udric does to a home. A considered touch. A quiet but undeniable presence. Not loud, not overwhelming — just felt.

We believe every home deserves to be seen. Every room deserves to be felt. And every space — however small, however forgotten — deserves the kind of presence that makes it whole.

"Udeh — presence. The kind that doesn't shout, but is always, quietly, undeniably felt. Every home we touch is seen. Every space we dress is remembered."
— The Udric Philosophy
The Name

Every letter
carries weight.

Udric is not just a brand name. It is a declaration. The fusion of Udeh (presence) and Patric represents our belief that great design is always a collaboration — between vision and execution, between feeling and form.

Just as cream applied in the right place at the right time can reveal a person's natural beauty, our studio applies design with precision and intention — revealing the inherent beauty of every space we touch.

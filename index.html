Why Udric

Born from a name.
Built on meaning.

Udric was born from two intentional names — Udeh and Patric. In Igbo, Udeh means presence. Some call it "cream" — and that philosophy drives everything we do.

The beauty of cream is what portrays us. Just as cream rises and refines everything it touches, we bring that same energy to every space we work on. Interior design is more than furniture and paint — it is the feeling you get when you walk into a room and everything just makes sense. The light, the texture, the flow, the details. That is what we obsess over.

We stay on your project. We pour into it. We make it clean, intentional, and alive — spaces that are not just visually seen but deeply felt by everyone who enters.

At Udric, we believe your space should speak before you do. It should reflect who you are, what you value, and the standard you carry. Whether it is a home, an office, or a commercial space, we treat every project as a canvas — and we do not leave until it is something worth remembering.

We create difference.
